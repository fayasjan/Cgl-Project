How It Works

Now that you have an account, here's how GaitAuth works to authenticate you using your unique walking pattern:

  1. Gait Video Capture: When you sign in, you will be prompted to upload a short video of yourself walking. This video captures your unique gait characteristics.
  2. Feature Extraction: Advanced algorithms analyze the video to extract key features of your gait, such as stride length, walking speed, and joint movements.
  3. Gait Pattern Matching: The extracted features are compared against the gait pattern stored in our secure database during your registration.
  4. Authentication Decision: If the gait features match closely with your stored pattern, the user is authenticated; otherwise, access is denied or additional verification is requested.
